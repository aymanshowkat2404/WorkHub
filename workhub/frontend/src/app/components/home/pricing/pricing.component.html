Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate will be applied immediately. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you'll have access to all features of the plan you choose during the trial period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for annual Enterprise plans, we can also accept bank transfers.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer a 25% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status to get this discount applied to your account.
